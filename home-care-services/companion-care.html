Elder Home Care's Companion Care Solutions
Having a friendly and trusted companion makes every day at home brighter. Children of aging parents often feel guilty for not being able to spend enough quality time together. At Elder Home Care , we have companions who enjoy spending time with your elderly loved ones. They can also perform light housekeeping tasks, assist in routine exercise and facilitate socializing with peers.
Studies have shown that human companionship benefits a senior’s physical and cognitive health, along with their overall sense of well-being. To put your mind at ease, Elder Home Care is a fully bonded and insured provider, and all of our caregivers are thoroughly screened beforehand. We will carefully match your loved one up with a friendly companion that’s uniquely qualified for their personality and interests who will engage them in healthy activities.
