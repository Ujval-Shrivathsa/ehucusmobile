Employment Information

Elder Home Care specializes in offering exceptional care to each client in all stages of life. We provide services to clients in private homes, assisted living communities, hospitals, skilled nursing facilities, and rehabilitation centers. We are actively looking for compassionate COMPANIONS, PCAs, CNAs, HHAs, LPNs, and RNs who desire to help our clients remain independent and have their best life now!

Requirements

All applicants must be able to provide the following information:

  • HHA Certification
  • Valid Photo ID
  • Recent Physical (within 12 months)
  • Two reference letters (1 Professional & 1 Personal)
