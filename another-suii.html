Trusted Senior Care

Elder Home Care is your partner in providing trustworthy, compassionate home care services to seniors living in Nassau, Suffolk, Queens, Manhattan, Westchester, Abington, PA and the greater Philadelphia, PA and Austin, TX areas.

Our experienced caregivers are trained to provide professional and reliable care in the same respectful and friendly manner that they would treat their own family members.

Elder Home Care understands having a caregiver in your loved one’s home is a decision that requires trust. Our goal is to build relationships with our clients based on the highest quality of care and compassionate listening to serve seniors. Our commitment is to help your aging loved ones maintain their well-being, independence and comfort.
